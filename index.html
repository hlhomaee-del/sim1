Weak area review cards

These cards are built from the categories you missed. Students can flip, review, and mark whether they understand the concept.

What students should scan first

  1. What type of plan is this, and what does that plan usually control most strictly?
  2. Is the service in-network, out-of-network, or possibly subject to an emergency exception?
  3. Is the clue pointing to a referral problem, an authorization problem, or neither?
  4. Was the patient active on the date of service?
  5. Is there a coordination-of-benefits issue hiding in the registration notes?
  6. Is this a true denial, a rejection, or just patient responsibility?
  7. Does the code point to invalid information, missing information, network rules, timing, or benefits processing?
